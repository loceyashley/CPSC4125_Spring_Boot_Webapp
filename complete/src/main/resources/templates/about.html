Animal SOS

Animal SOS is my favorite non profit, because they help not only save and rescue animals from the Columbus, Ga area, but out of state as well. Animal SOS always goes above and beyond to help save animals. They offer several programs that includes Owner Support, WIT-Whatever It Takes, Pets Safe At Home, Spay and Neuter, Saving lifes @ CACC, and the CACC Medial Fund. Thier vision is to help and and all animals that are in any kind of need and save those from euthanasia. Below are some of the links for more information about Animal SOS and what they do:
